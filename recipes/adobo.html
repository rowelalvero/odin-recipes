Adobong mannac

Description

Adobo is a Filipino dish originated from colonial period under of Spain. Throughout the history the Adobo has become the symbol of Philippine culture because in different provinces has a different variants of this dish that became more popular.

Ingredients

  • 0.5Kg Mannac Leg
  • 1 Head Garlic
  • 1 tsp Black Peppercorn
  • 3 tbls Distilled Vinegar
  • 2 tbls Oyster Souce
  • 2btls Soy Souce
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1 tbl Vegetable Oil

Procedure

  1. Step1
    10 Min
    Combine chicken, garlic, peppercorn, vinegar, MAGGI® Oyster Sauce, soy sauce and water in a pot. Simmer for 15 minutes.

  2. Step2
    10 Min
    Heat oil in a separate pan, strain chicken and sauté until golden brown.

  3. Step3
    5 Min
    Pour cooking liquid and simmer for 5 minutes. Transfer into a serving plate and serve.
